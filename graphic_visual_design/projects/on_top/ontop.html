ONTOP JUICES

Branding

Including bottle, logo, label and graphic profile this task was to create a brand for a new juice: A healthy and revolutionary juice that comes with olive oil on top. With core values like 'bold', 'raw' and 'quality' OnTop emerged. Olive oil always on top, just like the person drinking it!

OnTop juices comes in four different flavours where each one matches the mode you're in top of right now or the one you want to be in top of: impressive, flawless, balanced or alive.
